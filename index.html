Introducing Maji H2o

Maji H2O is a software application meant to enable Nairobi residents to easily identify water bowser suppliers in a timely manner and enable them to have water delivered to them more efficiently and effectively using modern day technologies.

WhyMaji H20

Maji H20 is a system that is meant to help residents get water and water bowser owners and drivers get relevant reports that enables them stand out from the rest of the market. Here are some of the key reasons as to why we consider Maji H2O to be a game changer in this industry.

Scheduledand On demand Ordering

Corporates can schedule their orders and at the same time its possible to make instant orders and they can keep track of their orders.


Real timeReporting

Based on our research, at least an individual owned three bowsers. Therefore it is important for them to be on the know on the performance of this vehicles. Trips taken, driver ratings and so on..

Keepingcustomers informed

Customers will be provided with information such as water sources, water prices and the feedback on drivers from their previous deliveries. This information would be key with enabling this customers to order water from this drivers.

Search For Bowsers

Customers can search for bowsers based on their current or preferred location. They will View Available service providers and information on their water sources and prices from where they can make their order or opt to schedule one.

Accept Request

The driver can accept a request and from where an order is received and created.

Realtime reporting

Payment
